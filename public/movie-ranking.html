Top Movies of All Time According To Brandon

My Best 3 Movies.

Spirited Away

The story telling in this movie was able to engage not only children but adults too.

Get Out

This film captured disturbing/uncomfortable emotion and tied humor towards the end tieing everything together.

Howl's Moving Castle

This is yet another animated film that at first not appear to have much dialogue. However you are distracted by how cinematic the scenes are.
